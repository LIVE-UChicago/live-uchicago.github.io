Are you a gifted individual looking for special opportunities?

Join us at the Learning Institute for the Very Enlightened at the University of Chicago.

The Learning Institute for the Very Enlightened (L.I.V.E.), a gifted school founded by Dr. Ledroptha Curtain, is conducting research at the University of Chicago this spring. We are looking for extraordinarily minded people to lead The Improvement by participating in an immersive experiment regarding neural responses to transmedia stimuli.

Experiments begin in the lobby of the Media Arts, Data, and Design Center on the first floor of John Crerar Library. They will be during the evening of Thursday, April 20 and the afternoon of Saturday, April 22 and last around 40 minutes.
